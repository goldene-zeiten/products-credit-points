======================
Products Credit Points
======================

:Extension key:

:Package name:

----

A credit-points loyalty programme for the Products shop system: customers earn points on the orders
they place and spend them at checkout for a discount, reached entirely through EXT:products_core's
loyalty and product-listing seams.
